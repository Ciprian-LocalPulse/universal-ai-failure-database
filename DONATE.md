# 💰 Donation Policy — Universal AI Failure Database

> **Full transparency. Zero overhead obfuscation. 100% toward research.**

---

## Allocation Policy

**100% of all donations received through this repository** are allocated exclusively to:

| Allocation Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **Research continuation** | Time, infrastructure, and tooling for ongoing AI failure documentation and analysis |
| **Open-access dissemination** | Hosting, archival (Zenodo, arXiv), and distribution of findings without paywalls |
| **Simulation infrastructure** | Compute costs for reproducible failure reproduction, model testing, and large-scale evaluation runs |
| **Academic review & conference costs** | Peer-review submission fees, conference registration, and travel for presenting findings |

No portion of donations is used for administrative overhead, marketing, or non-research purposes.

---

## Why Donate?

This project is maintained independently. There is no institutional funding, no venture capital, and no subscription model. The infrastructure and research time are sustained entirely through community support.

Your donation directly enables:

- Expanding the database to cover more models, more failure modes, and more languages
- Publishing structured findings in peer-reviewed venues for maximum academic impact
- Maintaining free, unrestricted access for all researchers globally
- Running reproducibility experiments on new model releases
- Presenting findings at venues such as NeurIPS, ICML, IEEE S&P, and ACL

## Institutional Support

Organizations (universities, research labs, AI companies) wishing to formally support this project may do so via:

- **Named sponsorship**: Your institution's name and logo in the README and CONTRIBUTORS file
- **Dataset co-authorship**: For substantial dataset contributions, co-authorship on resulting publications
- **Advisory collaboration**: For significant funding, structured collaboration agreements with publication credit

Contact via [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/yourusername/universal-ai-failure-database/discussions) to discuss institutional arrangements.

## Acknowledgement Preferences

Donors who wish to be acknowledged may open an Issue or Discussion with the label `acknowledgement` after donating, providing:
- Name or pseudonym (or "Anonymous")
- Affiliation (optional)
- Preferred acknowledgement format

Those who prefer to remain completely anonymous need take no action.

## Transparency Commitment

Annual summaries of funding received and allocation will be published in this repository under `docs/funding-reports/`. All amounts will be reported in aggregate to preserve donor privacy while maintaining public accountability.
